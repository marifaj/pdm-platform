Prepared files for windowed ML training and inference

Created from:
- idle_20260414(2).csv
- load_20260414(2).csv

Assumptions applied:
- factory_id = factory-001
- machine_id = machine-001
- device_id = esp32-001
- operating_state = idle or load based on source file
- label = normal for both source files

Outputs:
1. telemetry_training_combined_windowed.csv
   Full training dataset with required identity columns plus operating_state and label.
2. telemetry_training_combined_minimal.csv
   Minimal version containing only the columns required by the trainer.
3. app_windowed_inference.py
   Windowed inference service with MQTT input/output and no SQLite writes.
4. train_window_model.py
   Trainer that produces if_window_model.joblib compatible with app_windowed_inference.py
